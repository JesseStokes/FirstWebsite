Steel has a rich history that started over sixty years ago.

1965
Joe Steel has an idea to make a restaurant using quality products and ingredients only from his family farm.
1966
Joe opens his small restaurant to praise from everyone in town.
1970
After receiving numerous awards Steel gets remodeled into the fine dining destination it is known for today.
1990
Joe Steel Jr. takes over operation and establishes a working relationship with an organic fish farm in Alaska.
