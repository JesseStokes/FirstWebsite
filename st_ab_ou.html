At Steel we have always had one goal, to deliver the very best to our clients through top notch quality food and services. Even back in 1966 when Joe Steel opened the doors for the first time the highest priority was on the client experience. For over 60 years Steel has delivered on the promise of fine dining while winning numerous awards along the way.
